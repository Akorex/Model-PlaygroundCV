Welcome to Model Playground

This is where the Computer Vision models built will be tested in an interactive manner with the help of Flask framework. Another web app will be built to focus on the tasks in the area of Natural Language Processing and also with ML on Tabular Data.

The models built and featured here are quite simple and would be updated with more helpful and complex ones. The models are neural networks which would perform the task of classification, segmentation and even image captioning. They were built with the use of the wonderful Machine Learning library, TensorFlow, and other frameworks/libraries. The datasets used for training and the training notebooks are featured in each project task directory on the GitHub page where this simple web app is hosted.

The projects present on this model playground are:

  • Cats and Dogs
The Cats and Dogs page will allow you to upload the picture of a dog or a cat and it'll tell you which it is.
